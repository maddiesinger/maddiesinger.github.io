About our City

Keller is nestled in the heart of the Dallas Fort Worth Metroplex. Keller was settled in the early 1850's. It was founded when the Texas Pacific Railroad would extend north of Fort Worth. The city became a stop along the railroad. Originally called Athol it was renamed after a foreman on the railroad John C. Keller.
